Experience.

Software Developer Apprentice.

For 2 years, I worked as a software developer apprentice at the Ryerson Transmedia Zone. Since I first joined back in April 2019, I have had the privilege to work with two resident teams — Moziaq and Earbank.

During my time with Mozaiq, I conducted research on web technologies, artificial intelligence, and software capable of producing a more robust avatar customization plugin for existing stock animation platforms. Additionally, I built a prototype of the plugin on Unity.

During my time with Earbank, I designed, programmed, and maintained a dynamic web application for uploading audio files and generating metadata. I also incorporated a login and database system to ensure only authorized users had the ability to upload audio files.

A mock-up of the web application can be found in my Personal Project section below.

Private Mathematics Tutor.

Currently, I work as a private mathematics tutor with TutorBright. I love teaching my students the beauty of mathematics through real life examples and applications. I make every effort to help my students understand the lessons they are taught at school, learn fundamental concepts, and build an overall solid foundation.

In addition to teaching mathematics, I also provide guidance beyond their curriculum such as building their confidence and improving their work ethic — enabling them to meet any of their personal or academic goals.

As my experience in tutoring grew, I soon realized I wanted to go even further beyond and help my students have a way to further study, learn, or review certain topics in math — even if i'm not there! This led to the creation of Giordan’s Notebook — An online resource for various elementary & high school topics in Math.

A link for the website can be found in my Personal Project section below.

Youth Hub Tutor.

In my spare time, I volunteer as a mathematics and science Youth Hub tutor at the Toronto Public Library. I also collaborate with library staff and other tutors to help create a more welcoming and inclusive environment!
